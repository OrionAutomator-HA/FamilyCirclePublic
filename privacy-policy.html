Privacy Policy - Family Circle

Effective date: 4 June 2026 · Last updated: 2 July 2026
App: Family Circle (com.familycircle.app) · Android

1. Summary

Family Circle is a shared family calendar and household helper. You sign in with Google, join or create a family circle, and use calendars, notes, recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists with people you invite.

  • We do not sell your data or show ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs in the app.
  • Your data is stored on Supabase (cloud database and file storage).
  • Some data is private to you; some is shared with your circle based on settings you choose.
  • Store price tips use prices your family logs - not live supermarket APIs.

5. Google user data

Family Circle's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account ID, email address, and display name to authenticate you and create your Family Circle account. We do not use this information for advertising.

Optional Google Calendar sync (calendar.readonly)

If you choose Home → Calendar sync → Google Calendar and tap Allow Google Calendar access, we request read-only access with the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly. We use this access only to:

  • List your Google calendars so you can choose which to link
  • Read event titles, descriptions, start/end times, all-day flags, and recurrence from calendars you explicitly link
  • Display those events inside Family Circle on a private calendar visible only to you within your circle

We do not create, modify, or delete events in Google Calendar. Google Calendar data is not sold, used for advertising, or shared with data brokers. Imported event data is stored in our Supabase backend solely to provide this optional sync feature. Other circle members do not see your imported Google calendars unless you separately share events on a family calendar.

You can revoke access at any time by unlinking calendars in the app, signing out, or removing Family Circle in your Google Account permissions.

We use written terms with processors where required, including data-processing terms offered by our providers. We do not sell personal data.
