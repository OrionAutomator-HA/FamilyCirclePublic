Family Circle

Android app · Package com.familycircle.app
Developer: Orion Automator

What is Family Circle?
Family Circle is a shared family calendar and household helper for Android. Sign in with Google, create or join a private family circle, and coordinate everyday life together.

Purpose of the app

Family Circle helps households stay organised in one place. Members of a circle can:

  • Share and manage family calendars and events, including reminders and optional Google Calendar sync
  • Keep personal and shared notes visible only to the right people
  • Save recipes, plan meals, and build shopping lists
  • Track store prices and weekly budgets using prices your family logs
  • Invite trusted family members with a private invite code

The app does not show ads, does not sell your data, and does not use third-party analytics SDKs. Data is stored securely on Supabase and shared only within circles you join.

Who it is for

Family Circle is for families and households who want a simple, private way to share schedules and household tasks. You need a Google account to sign in. The app is not directed at children under 13.
