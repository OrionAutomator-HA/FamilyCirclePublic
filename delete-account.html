Delete your Family Circle account

App: Family Circle (com.familycircle.app) · Android
Developer: Orion Automator
Last updated: 4 June 2026

You can request permanent deletion of your Family Circle account and the personal data we hold about you. This page explains how to request deletion and what happens to your data.

How to request account deletion
  1. Send an email from the same Google address you use to sign in to Family Circle.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my Family Circle account.
  3. In the message, confirm that you want your account and associated data deleted.
  4. We will verify your identity using that email address and process your request.
Email Orion Automator to delete my account

Contact: orionautomatordev@gmail.com

We aim to confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days of verification.

What you can do in the app first (optional)

Before or instead of a full account deletion, you can:

  • Sign out (Settings) — removes local cached data on your device; cloud data remains.
  • Leave a circle (My circles) — removes your membership; you lose access to that circle’s data.
  • Delete your content — remove events, notes, recipes, and shopping items you created where the app allows delete.

These steps do not delete your sign-in account or all personal data we store. For full deletion, use the email request above.

Data that is deleted

When we process a confirmed account deletion request, we delete or anonymise:

DataAction
Your sign-in account link (Google / Supabase auth)Deleted
Circle memberships and profile (display name, avatar colour, role)Deleted
Personal notesDeleted
Private calendars and their eventsDeleted
Google Calendar / iCal sync connections you createdDeleted; imported events removed where applicable
Recipe photos you uploadedDeleted from storage
Local app cache on your deviceRemoved when you sign out or uninstall the app

Data that may be kept

DataWhy it may be kept
Shared notes, family calendar events, recipes, shopping list entries, or prices visible to other circle membersMay remain so other family members are not disrupted. We can delete your authored shared content on request if you specify this in your email.
Backups and server logsMay be retained up to 30 days for security and disaster recovery, then purged automatically.
Records we must keep by lawOnly if required (e.g. legal or tax obligations); otherwise deleted.

After deletion

  • You will not be able to sign in to Family Circle with the same account unless you create a new account.
  • Other members may still see content you shared in a circle unless you asked us to remove it in your deletion request.
  • Deleting your Family Circle account does not delete your Google account.
